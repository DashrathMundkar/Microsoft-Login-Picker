Privacy Policy — Microsoft Login Picker

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Overview

Microsoft Login Picker is a Chrome extension that forces Microsoft/Azure login pages to display the account selection screen. It operates entirely locally within your browser.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information. No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind is used.

How It Works

The extension adds a single URL query parameter (prompt=select_account) to Microsoft login requests. It does not read, intercept, or store any credentials, tokens, cookies, or form data.

Permissions

The extension requires access to Microsoft login domains solely to modify the login URL. No data from these domains is read or transmitted externally.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not communicate with any external servers or third-party services.

Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
